SPAGHETTI CARBONARA

This classic Italian pasta dish is made with eggs, cheese, bacon, and black pepper.



INGREDIENTS:

Spaghetti

Eggs

Parmesan cheese

Bacon or pancetta

Black pepper



INSTRUCTIONS:

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated Parmesan cheese.

Cook bacon until crispy, then add cooked spaghetti to the pan with bacon fat.

Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly until creamy.

Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
